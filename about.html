Our Story

A LEGACY OF CULINARY EXCELLENCE

Since 1895

Le Majestic opened its doors in 1895, founded by renowned French chef Auguste Beaumont. What began as a humble establishment has blossomed into one of the world's most celebrated fine dining destinations.

Our Philosophy

We believe that dining is an art form. Every dish that leaves our kitchen is a carefully crafted masterpiece, combining the finest ingredients with innovative techniques and timeless traditions.

Commitment to Excellence

Our dedication to perfection has earned us 3 Michelin stars for five consecutive years. We source ingredients from local artisans and international suppliers who share our passion for quality.
